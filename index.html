Not every engagement is the right fit. We only accept projects where we can confidently deliver measurable impact.

During discovery, we assess your business model, technical foundation, competitive landscape, and team dynamics to identify high-value opportunities. If we can't clearly see where to move the needle, we'll tell you upfront.

You get: Honest assessment and a clear go/no-go decision based on real revenue potential.

Integration & Planning

We don't operate in a silo. We integrate directly into your existing teams—engineering, content, marketing—and build our SEO roadmap around your workflows and priorities.

Our planning adapts to how you work, whether that's JIRA sprints, Slack channels, or weekly standups. The roadmap is phased, prioritized by impact, and designed to work with the resources you already have.

You get: A custom SEO roadmap that fits your team, not a one-size-fits-all playbook.

Implementation & Maintenance

We oversee execution of the SEO roadmap from start to finish. That means writing tickets, QA-ing work, monitoring performance, and removing friction wherever it appears.

This isn't set-it-and-forget-it. We stay embedded in your operations, course-correcting in real time and optimizing what's working while phasing out what's not.

You get: Continuous strategic oversight with accountability baked in at every step.

— 07 · Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Why a solo practice instead of an agency?
Enterprise SEO engagements at agencies typically pass through account managers to junior execution teams. Here, the senior strategist who scopes the work is the same person executing it. No handoffs, no learning curve on your account, no dilution of strategic judgment through layers.
What does “embedded” mean in practice?
I work directly within your team's tools, channels, and cadences — attending relevant standups, with access to your analytics and CMS, operating as a senior member of your marketing team. Not an outside vendor submitting tickets through a portal.
How do you approach GEO and AI search?
Generative Engine Optimization is woven into every engagement — content structure, entity authority, citation positioning. It's a natural extension of senior-level SEO strategy, not a separate productized service with its own price tag.
What is the minimum engagement?
Three months at 32, 48, or 64 hours per month. Organic growth compounds over time — shorter engagements don't give the work room to deliver meaningful, measurable results.
What does it cost?
$200 per hour. Engagements start at 32 hours/month ($6,400) with a 3-month minimum. Most run 6–12 months. Exact scope gets set during discovery.
When do results typically land?
Measurable traffic movement usually shows in 90–120 days. Compounding organic growth — the durable kind — typically takes 6 months to pattern. I give a specific forecast for your situation during discovery, tied to what we're actually working with.
